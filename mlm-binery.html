Understanding The Binary Compensation Plan

The binary MLM plan is designed to encourage teamwork and balanced growth. Each distributor can have only two front-line distributors—a left leg and a right leg. As your organization grows beneath these two legs, you earn commissions based on the volume of sales from both sides.
